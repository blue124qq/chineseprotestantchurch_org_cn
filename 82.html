Anhui CC Holds Ordination Ceremony in Hefei
On November 17, 2020, Anhui CC organized an ordination ceremony at the Church of Hefei City. Six pastors were ordained during the ceremony, namely Gu Ying (female), Wang Guangling (female), Zhang Huizhi (female), Zhang Xingbo, An Xiuyun (female), Tian Bao. There were also 11 newly-ordained elders including Sun Guocang, Liu Zufeng (female), Wang Xianxia (female), Cao Ying (female), Zhou Fenglian (female), Li Guangjun, Wang Baolan (female), Chen Guo’an, Zhu Dailin, Gao Deming, Xiao Hong (female).

Zhuhai Christian Community Makes Donations for a Minority Group in Yunnan Province
From October 19th to 24th, 2020, Rev. Fan Hong’en, Chairman of Guangdong TSPM, who is also the President of Zhuhai CC and Chairman of Zhuhai TSPM led a four-person team heading towards Lushui City of Nujiang Lisu Autonomous Prefecture in Yunnan Province for poverty-relief donations and surveys.

Churches Conduct Activities on the Christian Charity Day
The Christian Charity Day falls on the third Saturday and Sunday of every October. CCC&TSPM called upon churches, brothers and sisters across the country to participate in the two-day worship activities, themed by “Our Thoughts Are with the Poor, Enter the Rich Land”, and encouraged them to make donations for charitable activities of all sorts by local churches.

Yunnan CC&TSPM Organizes Cultural Exchanges among Christian Ethnic Minorities
To proactively explore and carry forward the path towards the indigenization and contextualization of Christianity in China with Yunnan characteristics, enhance the cultural exchanges among different ethnic groups in the Christian community, and advance talent cultivation, Yunnan CC&TSPM organized cultural exchanges among Christian ethnic minorities in Kunming on October 20, 2020.

Guangdong CC&TSPM Ordains an Elder
On the morning of October 17, 2020, Guangdong CC&TSPM conducted an ordination ceremony in Hecheng Church, Gaoming District, Foshan City, ordaining Director of Hecheng Church Ms. Wang Fuhua as an elder.

Shandong CC&TSPM Ordains Pastors and Elders
​On September 27th, 2020, Shandong CC&TSPM held an ordination ceremony in Qingzhi Church, Weifang City. Feng Xiangke, Xu Qiufen (female), Wang Jianming (female), Wang Qingyun (female), Wang Lishu (female), Zhang Zeyan (female) and Yang Shichang were ordained as pastors; Xu Xinmei (female) and Wang Changyu were ordained as elders.

Thirty-Six Preachers Ordained in Jilin
On September 15, 2020, Jilin CC&TSPM conducted an ordination in Xiwu Road Church in Nanguang District, Changchun City. 16 preachers from Changchun City, Songyuan City, Siping City and Gongzhuling City were ordained as pastors.
2020-09-30
Young Fellow Workers Ordained in Guangzhou
On the morning of September 19, Guangzhou Dongshan Church held an ordination ceremony to ordain 20 young fellow workers from eight cities of Guangdong Province. Up to 300 people participated in the ceremony, consisting of church leaders from 21 cities of Guangdong Province, and pastoral staff as well as fellow workers from churches and church-affiliated fellowships in Guangzhou City.
2020-09-25
Activities Held to Celebrate the 70th Anniversary of Three-Self Patriotic Movement of the Protestant Churches in China
On August 21, 2020, Tianjin CC&TSPM held a commemoration of the 70th anniversary of Three-Self Patriotic Movement of the Protestant churches in China & symposium on further promoting the indigenization and contextualization of Christianity in China. The pastoral staff from churches in Tianjin attended the symposium. Rev. Xu Xiaohong, chairman of National TSPM, was also invited to the symposium.
