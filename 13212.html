CCC&TSPM Provides Supplies to Volunteers of the Sub-district Office of the Bund, Shanghai
2020-03-04 Source: this site
A+
A-

In the battle of the COVID-19 prevention and control, a large number of volunteers have been committed to working together with local communities in building a forefront protective wall to defend the virus.

 


On March 2, 2020, CCC&TSPM donated lots of supplies including personal protective equipment to the Sub-district Office of the Bund in Huangpu District, Shanghai, in an attempt to support and care for the frontline volunteers of the community.
